Wathiq is a modern and secure e-invoicing platform designed to digitally empower businesses. It helps organizations digitize invoicing, strengthen compliance, and drive growth through streamlined invoice issuance, VAT handling, and real-time financial insights.

### What Wathiq delivers

- **Digitize Invoicing** — a clean, end-to-end flow from issuance to archiving, with invoices as structured data rather than flat PDFs.
- **Strengthen Compliance** — built-in VAT handling, invoice validation, and regulator-ready formats so businesses are compliant by default.
- **Empower Your Business** — real-time dashboards, paid-vs-unpaid visibility, and revenue insights turning invoicing data into actionable signal.

Wathiq is available on GitHub at [github.com/Khalil-am/Wathiq](https://github.com/Khalil-am/Wathiq).
