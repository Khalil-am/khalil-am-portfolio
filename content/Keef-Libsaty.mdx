Keef Libsaty is a fashion recommendation platform that leverages AI to provide users with instant suggestions based on uploaded images or links. Ideal for fashion enthusiasts, Keef Libsaty helps users discover similar items available for purchase, enhancing the shopping experience.

## Features

- **Image-Based Search**: Users can upload photos to find similar fashion items.
- **AI Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions based on style, color, and brand preferences.
- **E-commerce Integration**: Links directly to online stores, making it easy to purchase recommended items.
