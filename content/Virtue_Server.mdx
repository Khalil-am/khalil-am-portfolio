Virtue is an AI-based mental health platform that helps users improve their well-being. By offering personalized tools and securely managing data, Virtue creates a supportive environment for users to address mental health concerns with discretion and safety.

## Key Aspects

- **Tailored Mental Health Tools**: Provides users with tools and resources customized to their mental health needs.
- **Secure Data Management**: Ensures user privacy with robust data encryption and access controls.
- **Comprehensive Support**: Offers guidance for common mental health challenges such as stress and anxiety.
