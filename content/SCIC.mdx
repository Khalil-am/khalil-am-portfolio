The SCIC project is an innovative platform designed to provide actionable insights into supply chain management. Leveraging AI, SCIC enables businesses to monitor, analyze, and optimize their supply chains, reducing costs and improving operational efficiency.

## Features

- **Predictive Analytics**: Uses machine learning to forecast demand, inventory needs, and potential bottlenecks.
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Provides a live view of the supply chain, helping businesses react to changes swiftly.
- **Cost Optimization Tools**: Identifies cost-saving opportunities through data analysis and simulation.
