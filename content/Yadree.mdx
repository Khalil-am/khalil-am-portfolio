Yadree is a comprehensive business intelligence consultant system that utilizes AI and machine learning to assist businesses in real-time decision-making. Featuring SQLFlow for natural language queries, BIAC simplifies complex data analytics, making it a valuable tool for enterprises seeking strategic insights.

## Highlights

- **AI-Driven Data Analysis**: Offers real-time analytics with machine learning models for predictive insights.
- **SQLFlow Integration**: Supports natural language queries, simplifying data retrieval and visualization.
- **Interactive Dashboards**: Provides dynamic data visualizations to track business performance and KPIs.
